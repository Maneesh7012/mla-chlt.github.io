CHLT Features you can get
Our toolbox is comprised of a dashboard with site-specific climate snapshot and weather forecast, a Risk Analysis Program (RAP), and a compilation of cattle behaviour information. The toolbox sets a world standard for the management of heat in lot fed animals.
Site Dashboard

The site dashboard assists in giving cattle headload statistics. It also displays other critical site information.

Alerts

The alerts notify the manager as well as the additional users of the heat load index and AHLU.

Forecast

The tool displays two sorts of forecasts: heat load forecasts and weather forecasts. This allows the user to plan ahead of time.
